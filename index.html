About me

I am a dedicated and ambitious Junior Full-Stack Web Developer with a background in the Portuguese army. Having served for six years, including three years representing Portugal in the NATO HQ and European Military Committee based in Belgium, I have cultivated valuable qualities such as discipline, teamwork, and adaptability.

Seeking a fresh career path, I committed myself to the Le Wagon 9-week Web Development bootcamp, where I acquired a solid foundation and practical experience in various web development technologies. I am proficient in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and frameworks like Ruby on Rails. Additionally, I have hands-on experience using version control systems like GitHub and deploying web applications on platforms such as Heroku.

As a junior developer, I understand the importance of continuous learning and thrive in a supportive and collaborative environment. I am passionate about problem-solving, creativity, and staying up-to-date with the latest industry trends. Contributing to dynamic projects and expanding my expertise in web development excites me.

With a strong work ethic, attention to detail, and a willingness to learn, I am confident in my ability to make positive contributions to any team. I am open to new opportunities and challenges that will allow me to grow and enhance my skills as a Junior Full-Stack Web Developer.

Projects

AstroRide

This collaborative endeavor involved a team of four individuals, focused on creating a replica of Airbnb's distinctive features, but with a unique twist of renting out spaceships. Within this project, users have the capability to both list their own spaceships for rent, specifying available dates and prices, as well as rent spaceships from other users for specific dates. As a Full-Stack developer, my responsibilities encompassed a diverse range of tasks, including proficiency in Rails, Ruby, JavaScript, HTML, CSS, GitHub, and Heroku.


MoneyCounter

A remarkable project completed by a team of four individuals for the culminating assignment of the esteemed Le Wagon Bootcamp, this is a fully functional website designed to facilitate expense and income management on mobile devices. The platform offers comprehensive categorization capabilities, allowing users to efficiently organize their financial transactions. Moreover, it incorporates a captivating feature called Goals, enabling users to set and strive for specific financial objectives. Additionally, the inclusion of a unique feature named Groups empowers users to share expenses with friends, facilitating clear visibility into owed debts and outstanding amounts. As a Full-Stack developer, my responsibilities encompassed a diverse range of tasks, including proficiency in Rails, Ruby, JavaScript, HTML, CSS, GitHub, and Heroku.
